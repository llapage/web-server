Our Services

At Evergreen Oasis Landscaping, we offer a wide range of landscaping services to transform your outdoor space into a beautiful oasis. Our experienced team will work closely with you to understand your vision and bring it to life. Explore our services below:

Landscape Design

Create the perfect outdoor retreat with our custom landscape design services. From initial concept to final installation, we’ll work with you every step of the way to design a landscape that reflects your style and enhances the natural beauty of your property.

Lawn Care and Maintenance

Keep your lawn looking lush and healthy year-round with our comprehensive lawn care and maintenance services. From mowing and fertilizing to weed control and irrigation management, we’ll ensure your lawn remains vibrant and green.

Garden Installation and Maintenance

Add color and texture to your landscape with our garden installation and maintenance services. Whether you’re looking for vibrant flower beds, lush shrubbery, or serene water features, our team will create a garden that complements your home and brings joy year-round.

Landscape Design and Installation

Enhance your outdoor living space with our landscape design and installation services. From patios and walkways to retaining walls and fire pits, we’ll design and build durable and beautiful landscapes that extend your living area and add value to your property.

Tree and Shrub Care

Protect the health and beauty of your trees and shrubs with our expert care services. From pruning and trimming to disease management and pest control, we’ll ensure your trees and shrubs thrive for years to come.

Seasonal Clean-Up

Prepare your landscape for each season with our seasonal clean-up services. Whether it’s removing leaves and debris in the fall or prepping your garden for spring planting, we’ll keep your landscape looking its best year-round.
